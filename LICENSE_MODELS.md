# BlockSeer — Commercial Licensing Models

BlockSeer is sold under one of three commercial licensing models.
This page is a **non-binding overview** intended to help you decide
which model fits your situation. The full contractual terms,
pricing, support window, day-rate and migration rules are disclosed
after a signed NDA — see [`CONTACT.md`](./CONTACT.md).

---

## Which model fits your situation?

| Question | → Likely Model |
|---|---|
| You want to **own** BlockSeer outright and develop it further yourself, no further involvement from Stoffel999? | **Model A** |
| You want **exclusive use** of BlockSeer and the vendor to keep developing it for you (against fees you agree per update)? | **Model B** |
| You want a **single working licence** at the lowest entry price and accept that up to two other buyers may hold the same version in parallel? | **Model C** |
| Not sure yet? | Start with **Model C** — you can migrate to **B** or **A** later. |

## Notes on all models

- **Two-version support window (Models B and C):** the current version (n) receives full support, the previous version (n-1) receives security and critical-bug patches; older versions are out of support and an upgrade path is offered.
- **Cryptographic authenticity key:** each licensed deployment ships with a vendor-signed key file that the backend verifies at startup. This protects both the vendor (against forged copies) and the buyer (against tampered downloads).
- **Custom development at day-rate:** outside the included support, the vendor offers consultancy for custom features, third-pool integrations, performance tuning or training workshops.
- **Migration always possible:** Model C can be upgraded to Model B or Model A at any time by paying the price difference and signing a new commercial agreement.
